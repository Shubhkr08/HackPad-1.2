# HackPad-1.2
<img width="874" height="530" alt="Screenshot (36)" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b87a5a0a-a3bd-4344-a77f-f6b530384ad9" /><br>
My personal micro pad for general shortcuts and game development, I am using 3 x 3 keys matrix and 9 diode (so it just needs 6 gpio pins) and 3 extra keys to switch between three different channels , so in total the micro pad can have a total of 3x9 = 27 output. It is based on XIAO RP2040<br><br>
**BOM**<br>
**#**
XIAO RP2040<br>
**#**
12 x MX-Style switches <br>
**#**
12 x Blank DSA keycaps<br>
**#**
9 x Through-hole 1N4148 Diodes <br>
**#**
4 x  M3x16mm screws <br>
**#**
4 x  M3x5mx4mm heatset inserts <br>
